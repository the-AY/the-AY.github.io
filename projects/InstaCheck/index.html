Process Guide

  1. Open Instagram Settings > Accounts Center.
  2. Select Your information and permissions > Download your information.
  3. Choose Download or transfer information > Some of your information.
  4. Scroll down to find and select Followers and following.
  5. Select Format: JSON and Date Range: All time.
  6. Download the ZIP when ready, extract it, and find the JSON files in connections/followers_and_following/.

Privacy First: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server. This tool is open-source and secure.
